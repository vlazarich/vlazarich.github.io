What I Do

For more than 8 years I've been composing, producing, recording, mixing and mastering music using analog and virtual instruments. I've had experience of working with indie bands, social projects and filmmakers. My main strength is to carefully understand what client needs and to create sketches quickly.

Who I Am

I'm a songwriter, poet and musician. I play acoustic and electric guitar, keys, percussion and I can recreate any sound using Ableton Live. For 2 years I've had a classic guitar class and developed my skills to play jazz and bossa-nova. In 2020 I've finished the course "Getting Started with Music Theory" by Michigan State University at Coursera. I've never stopped learning songwriting, music recording and production. .

My Work

I've worked with film director Iryna Pravylo on her movie projects and with Congress of Cultural Activists on their "Crimean Tatars Audiobook". I've made music and wrote songs for Peredmova in such genres as Indie Pop, Drum & Bass, Electronic, House, Folk, World, Orchestral, Minimal, Experimental, Cinematic, Conceptual. My poems were included in the printed edition of zine Addenda Press #1: Poetic Kyiv.
