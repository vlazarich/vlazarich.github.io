What I Do

For more than 8 years I've been composing, writing lyrics, producing, recording, mixing and mastering music using analog and virtual instruments. I've had experience of working with indie bands, social projects and filmmakers. My main strength is to carefully understand what client needs and to create sketches quickly.

Who I Am

I'm composer and songwriter, poet and musician. I play acoustic and electric guitar, keys, percussion and I can recreate any sound using Ableton Live. For 2 years I've studied classic guitar and have developed my skills to play jazz and bossa-nova. In 2020 I've finished the course "Getting Started with Music Theory" by Michigan State University on Coursera. I've never stopped learning songwriting, music recording and production. In 2017 I've started a label Dobryi Bober Records.

My Work

I've worked with the film director Iryna Pravylo on her movie projects, with Congress of Cultural Activists on their "Crimean Tatars Audiobook". I've made music and wrote songs for Peredmova, Night Dew Call and KELBAS.

I'm experienced in such genres, as Indie Pop, Guitar, Drum & Bass, Electronic, House, Folk, World, Orchestral, Minimal, Shoegaze, Twee, Experimental, Cinematic, Conceptual.
