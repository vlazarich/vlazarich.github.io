What I Do

For more than 10 years I've been composing, writing lyrics, producing, recording, mixing and mastering music using analog and virtual instruments. I've worked with indie bands and artists, social projects and filmmakers. My main strength is to carefully understand what client needs and to create quick music sketches.

Who I Am

I'm composer and songwriter, musician and sound designer, mixing and mastering engineer. I play acoustic and electric guitar, keys, percussion and I can recreate any sound using Ableton Live. For 2 years I've studied classic guitar and have developed my skills to play jazz and bossa-nova. In 2017 I've started a label Dobryi Bober Records. In 2020 I've finished the course "Getting Started with Music Theory" by Michigan State University on Coursera. Now I'm an independent music producer, experienced in genres from indie pop and hip-hop to hardcore and spoken word.
